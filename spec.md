# Boston Bee Charged - Mobile EV Charging Service

## Overview
A comprehensive website for Boston Bee Charged, a mobile EV charging company that provides on-demand electric vehicle charging services. The application allows customers to learn about the service, schedule charging appointments, and contact the business.

## Core Features

### Homepage
- Professional header with company logo (realistic or tech-inspired design)
- Hero section highlighting the mobile EV charging service
- Mission statement and company overview
- Key benefits of mobile EV charging
- Service area information
- Call-to-action buttons directing to scheduling
- Professional footer with contact information and brand imagery

### Appointment Scheduling System
- Interactive booking form for mobile EV charging appointments
- Location selection (address input or map integration)
- Vehicle type selection (various EV models and battery capacities)
- Date and time picker for preferred charging appointment
- Contact information collection (name, phone, email)
- Appointment confirmation system
- Service duration and pricing information

### Business Information
- Contact details (phone, email, business address)
- Service hours and availability
- Coverage area information
- Pricing structure for different vehicle types
- FAQ section about mobile EV charging

### User Interface
- Clean, professional design reflecting the EV/tech industry
- Responsive layout optimized for both mobile and desktop
- Intuitive navigation between homepage and scheduling
- Professional color scheme consistent with brand identity
- Easy-to-use booking interface
- Clear call-to-action elements

## Backend Requirements
- Store appointment bookings with customer details, location, vehicle type, and scheduled time
- Manage appointment availability and scheduling conflicts
- Store customer contact information
- Handle appointment confirmation and management
- Provide endpoints for creating and retrieving appointments

## Technical Requirements
- Responsive design for mobile and desktop users
- Form validation for appointment booking
- Professional presentation suitable for business website
- Integration of existing logo assets in header and footer
- Clean, modern interface reflecting EV industry standards
